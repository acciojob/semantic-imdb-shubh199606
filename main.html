Plot Summary

Inception is a science-fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a thief with the ability to enter people's dreams and extract information. He is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can perform an almost impossible task: planting an idea in someone's subconscious, a process known as inception.

Main Actors

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
